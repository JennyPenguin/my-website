Memories Collider

Origin:

This was my very first VR project. Originally, I planned on creating a cherry blossom forest that people can walk in. I love cherry blossom and it's one of my life goals before I die to walk in a real one. After I shared this idea with my mom, she was very excited, and she told me that it would be great to use VR to not fulfill our life dreams but also life regrets. That's when we came up with the idea of a memory collider filled with cherry blossoms, roses, and many more together. The central goal is to fulfill one of my mom's biggest regret - not having a proper wedding - using scenes in VR.

Illuminate

Description:

This is a mystery puzzle game that I made during a Hackathon together with Jonathan Jiang, Eva, and Noveled.

Gameplay:

The player is a little bulb of light trying to escape the dark cave and reach the "illuminated" sky. The player can use the arrows keys or WASD to move around. They can use t to use objects and number keys to select objects in the inventory. There are multiple puzzles that needs to be solved.

Forest Terminator

Description:

This is the 3D Unity game that I made together with my friend Angela Cui.

Gameplay:

The player can use the arrows keys or WASD to move around. They can also move the mouse to control the camera view. The player must explore the magical forest and help save the animals from the danger. There is a full storyline in the game, and the player can talk to the animals to get clues to their next step. There is also a boss fight and challenges for the player to overcome.

Solar Eclipse

Description:

This is platform game with differeent story endings. The player can explore diverse planets with varying enemies.

Gameplay

The player is an astronaut who must rescue some missing solders from space pirates. The player has to go through different planets in order to find the soliders. Each planet is a different level with different challenges.

Platform Runner

Description:

This was one of my early 2D games. I added character animations for the first time. It was incredibly fun to draw the vibrating character and add all the settings.

Gameplay:

The player can use the arrows keys or WASD to move around. They have to jump to the platform before it disappears and randomly move to another location. The player can triple jump. Once the player fall down the screen, they lose.

Asteroids

Description:

This is my remake of the classic aracde game Asteroids. This is the first time I added particle systems to my games.

Gameplay:

The player can use the arrows keys or WAD to move around. Asteroids will be randomly spawning. If the player gets hit by a asteroid, they lose a heart. Once they lose all 3 hearts, the game ends. The player may press the Space key to shoot at the enemies.

Clicker Game

Description:

This was one of my earlest 2D projects. It's a simple and relaxing clicking game.

Gameplay:

The player can click on the characters in the room while listening to relaxing background musics. When they reach a certain number of clicks, they can exchange clicks for items to decorate different environments.

Maze Runner

Description:

This was my first 2D projects. It's a simple platform game, but it's very hard to beat.

Gameplay:

The player can use the arrow keys or WASD to move around while avoiding the slime obstacles. If the player gets hit, they lose a heart. They have a total of 3 hearts. When the player lose, they can press the "R" key to restart the game. Reach the finish line to win.
